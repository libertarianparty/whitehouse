Inauguration of President John Q. Adams

On January 20, 1960 President John Quincy Adams was sworn into office.

He plans to make revolutionary changes, and bring peace to the American people.

President John Quincy Adams Inaugural address:

This is America, this is the beauty of it. For 171 years this tradition still stands. During my presidency we will strive and come out on top. I will stand up to the forces of evil in this world. I will make it my duty to represent the American people and prove that we can not be kicked around anymore. We will reduce taxes and debt. We will pull america back out of the hole. I am going to put the people back in power. Today is the day of America, Today is the day of you!

The White House
